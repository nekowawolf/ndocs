Andrena (DAWN)

DAWN aims to transform internet access similarly to how solar panels transformed electricity, enabling consumers to own, generate, and sell their own internet service. Now DAWN has launched a testnet, where we can use the Internet in normal mode and get points for it. Grass and Nodepay have a similar activity.

DAWN Guide

  1. Install the Dawn extension.
  2. Click the Register button in the extension and enter the code hzyzqj
    (you won't be able to log into the app without it):
  3. Confirm your registration at the mail.
  4. Open the extension again and log in to your account. If everything was successful, you will see a similar window:

    5. For more rewards, click Boost rewards. Here you can invite friends and complete tasks.
